1. Place all of the soup ingredients into a pressure cooker.

2. Place the lid on the cooker and make sure the pressure knob is in the SEALING position and use the +/- buttons until the display reads 1 minute.

3. When the cooker beeps to let you know it's finished, switch the pressure knob from the SEALING to the VENTING position, administering a quick release.

4. Remove the lid and serve immediately.
